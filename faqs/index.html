Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about the Kingston City Run 2025 event, registration, and more.

When is Kingston City Run 2025?

The event has been postponed following Hurricane Melissa. A new date will be announced once updated arrangements have been confirmed.

How do I register?

Click the "Register" link in the menu or visit the registration page to sign up online.

What race options are available?

You can choose from a 10K Run or a 5K Walk/Run/Wheel, suitable for all ages and abilities.

Where does the race start?

The event starts and finishes at Emancipation Park, Kingston.

Who benefits from this event?

All proceeds go towards charities supporting the homeless in Kingston, including Open Arms, Marie Atkins Night Shelter, and Alpha Boys School.

Will I get a medal?

Yes, all registered participants will receive a commemorative Kingston City Run medal.

Is parking available?

Limited parking is available near Emancipation Park. We encourage carpooling or using public transport where possible.

How can I get more information?

For more info, visit the Contact page or email us at kingstoncityrun@gmail.com.
