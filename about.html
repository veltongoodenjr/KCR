About Kingston City Run

More than a race—Kingston’s run for community, charity, and city pride!

Our Mission

Kingston City Run brings together athletes, families, and friends for a fun, purpose-driven experience—raising funds for the city’s homeless charities and building community spirit.

Our Story

  • Founded in 2013 by JHTA Kingston Chapter
  • J$21M+ raised for local charities
  • Thousands of participants annually
  • 9 years and counting!
Each year, runners, walkers, volunteers, and sponsors make Kingston City Run a citywide celebration with lasting impact.

Who We Are

Kingston City Run is organised by the Jamaica Hotel & Tourist Association (Kingston Chapter), powered by local sponsors, passionate volunteers, and a supportive community.
