Race Route & Stops

  • Start/Finish: Emancipation Park
  • Water Stations: Every 2.5km (look for the pirate flags!)
  • Cheer Zones: Parade Avenue, Devon House, Knutsford Blvd
  • Restrooms: Start/Finish & midway
  • Start Time: 6:30 AM (10K), 7:00 AM (5K)
  • Parking: New Kingston lots, Knutsford Blvd
  • Safety: Route marshals & medical tents on site
  • Prizes: Top finishers & best pirate costume!

Runner Essentials

  • Bring your bib and ID for packet pickup.
  • Water and aid stations are available along the route.
  • Pirate costumes are encouraged (prizes for best dressed!).
  • Follow all marshal and safety instructions.
  • Contact race organizers for accessibility needs.
