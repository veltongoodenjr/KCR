Race Info & Course Map

10K Run • 5K Walk/Run/Wheel
Sunday, November 2, 2025
Emancipation Park, Kingston

"Pirate’s Run di City" – Run Jamaica's most scenic, vibrant and musical city race!

Race Highlights

  • Experience Kingston’s scenic and iconic attractions, including Devon House, Kings House, Peter Tosh and Bob Marley Museums.
  • Choose your challenge: A competitive 10K or a 5K Walk/Run/Wheel—perfect for all ages, families, and teams.
  • Vibrant Pirate’s Week theme: Four fan zones (Fashion Zone, Devon House, Kings House, Reggae Zone) with music and exclusive costumes.
  • Cheer zones, aid stations, and finish line festivities for supporters and fans.
  • Support local charities: Open Arms Drop-in Centre, Marie Atkins Night Shelter, Alpha Boys School.

Course Overview & Map

  • Start/Finish: Emancipation Park
  • 5K Route: Knutsford Blvd, Trafalgar Rd, Hope Rd, Kings House Rd, Lady Musgrave, Devon House, back to Knutsford.
  • 10K Route: All 5K route plus Barbican Beach City, Paddington, Lady Musgrave, Devon House, Knutsford.
  • Water & Aid Stations: Every 2.5km, fully equipped medical support, cooling stations.
  • Start Times: 10K: 6:30 AM • 5K: 7:00 AM
  • Timing: Chip timing (gun & chip results), event sanctioned by JAAA.
*Route subject to minor changes. See [Official Course Map] for latest updates.
