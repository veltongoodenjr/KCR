Kingston City Run 2025

Pirates Run Di City – Part of Kingston Pirate’s Week

Emancipation Park, Kingston • New date to be announced

Run, walk, or wheel for a cause! The Kingston City Run, now part of Kingston Pirate’s Week, brings together communities and corporations to help raise funds for Kingston’s most vulnerable. Choose between the thrilling 10K race or the family-friendly 5K Walk/Run/Wheel, and celebrate Jamaica’s pirate history with costume contests, entertainment, and more.

Donate Today!

Postponed

Due to the impact of Hurricane Melissa, the Kingston City Run (KCR) and all Kingston Pirates Week activities have been postponed. Our thoughts are with everyone affected by the storm. We wish you safety and a steady recovery across Jamaica.

A new date for the KCR will be announced soon as we work with our partners to finalise updated plans.

Thank you for your understanding and continued support as we rally to assist those most in need, especially our beneficiaries who serve the homeless community.

Why Join the Adventure?

Multiple Race Events

Take on the 10K run or join the accessible 5K Walk/Run/Wheel—open to all ages and abilities.

Kingston Pirate’s Week

Immerse yourself in the excitement of Kingston Pirate’s Week with parades, music, costumes, and city-wide events.

Help Raise for Charity

Proceeds support Kingston’s homeless charities including Open Arms, Marie Atkins Night Shelter, Food for the Poor, and Missionaries of the Poor.

Team & Family Fun

Corporate challenges, family teams, and community groups are all welcome. Celebrate teamwork and community spirit!

Dress Up & Win Prizes

Unleash your inner pirate—compete in the pirate costume contest for prizes and recognition!
