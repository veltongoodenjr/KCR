Every step you take at Kingston City Run supports real change for the homeless and at-risk communities in Kingston. Your participation helps fund shelters, food programmes, and vital support for those who need it most.

How Your Support Makes a Difference

Every registration fee, donation, and step on race day helps to:

  • Provide beds, meals, and medical aid for those living on the streets
  • Fund job training and social services for vulnerable youth
  • Support crisis response for families at risk of homelessness
  • Expand the reach of Kingston’s shelters and drop-in centres

Thank you for being part of the movement for positive change in Kingston!
