Help Raise for Kingston's Homeless

Your steps make an impact. Every registration helps fund shelters, meals, and hope for those who need it most.

Why We Run

Kingston City Run is committed to fighting homelessness and supporting the most vulnerable. Since 2013, over J$21 Million has been raised for charities making a real difference in the city. Join us to help expand their impact!

Charities You Help Support

Open Arms Drop-In Centre

Shelter, meals, and life skills for Kingston’s homeless community.

Marie Atkins Night Shelter

A safe haven and support for hundreds each year.

Alpha Boys School

Education and hope for at-risk youth & future musicians.

Charity partners are updated each year. Interested in partnering? Contact us!
