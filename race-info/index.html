Race Info & Course Map

10K Run • 5K Walk/Run/Wheel
Sunday, November 2, 2025
Emancipation Park, Kingston

"Pirate’s Run di City" – Run Jamaica's most scenic, vibrant and musical city race!

Race Highlights

Iconic Kingston Route

Devon House, Kings House, Bob Marley & Peter Tosh Museums—all along the course.

Choose Your Challenge

10K for competitive runners, 5K for all ages, walkers, wheelers, and families.

Pirate’s Week Theme

Fan zones, music, exclusive pirate costumes, and festival vibes.

Run for a Cause

Support Open Arms, Marie Atkins, Alpha Boys School, and more local charities.

Weekend Celebration

Parties, expo, cheer zones, awards, and a reggae party at the finish.

Comfort & Support

Multiple aid stations, cheer squads, and finish-line fun for all.

Course Overview

Start/Finish

Emancipation Park

5K Route

Knutsford Blvd → Trafalgar Rd → Hope Rd → Kings House Rd → Lady Musgrave → Devon House → Knutsford

10K Route

All 5K + Barbican Beach City → Paddington → Lady Musgrave → Devon House → Knutsford

Water & Aid Stations

Every 2.5 km, with medical & cooling support throughout.

Start Times

10K: 6:30 AM • 5K: 7:00 AM

Timing Method

Gun & chip results, sanctioned by JAAA.

*Route subject to minor changes.
